Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a separate app? +
No! Memorae works entirely within WhatsApp. Just add our contact and start messaging - no downloads or installations required.
Can I set recurring reminders? +
Yes! With our Pro and SuperNova plans, you can set daily, weekly, monthly, or custom recurring reminders that repeat automatically.
Is WhatsApp enough? +
Absolutely! Unlike complex task management tools, Memorae focuses specifically on reminders and works entirely through WhatsApp chat. It's simple, familiar, and effective.
How does Google Calendar sync work? +
Memorae can automatically add your reminders to Google Calendar and sync existing events. This keeps everything organized in one place while still getting WhatsApp notifications.
